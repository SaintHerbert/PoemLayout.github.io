Tuff Times

At first you think it's going to be ok
And think all will be ok.
But it creeps up on you with a thought.
You start to sink in your thoughts.
It raises your heart beat.
The damn thing won't go away!
It won’t let you be free.
It won’t even let you sleep.
You’ve got to get rid of it.
We all have our ways.
But they are not all the same.

The Hat

Hanging, sitting, flying
So many possibilities
Something so simple
In the wind, it flies away
On the wall it hangs
So many ways
On a head it sits
It keeps the hair calm
It can represent anything
Place, movement, sport
So many possibilities
colors, themes, and sizes
So many
It can be a collectors item
It can be a souvenir
It can be for a specific occasion
So many

The Relief

Finally, sleep
The feeling of being relaxed.
The feeling of sitting on the couch.
Or the feeling of the end of a work shift.
At night on your bed reading a book with a dimmed light.
It's over.
The feeling of being relieved.
It makes you feel lightweight.
Wanting to do things.
Having a good night's sleep.
Oh that great feeling.

Happy

You always want to aim for this feeling.
But sometimes it just won't come to you.
And when you get it.
Keep it.
As long as you can.
It lets you do things.
It's the feeling when you’re opening a present.
Or you’ve achieved something.
It’s when you’ve had a good day.
It's a good feeling.
All you’ve got to do is keep it.
